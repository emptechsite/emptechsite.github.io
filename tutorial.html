Harmonic Sequence Tutorial

A harmonic sequence is a sequence of numbers where the reciprocals of the terms form an arithmetic sequence.

In simple terms: if you flip each term (1 divided by the number), the new sequence has a constant difference.

Example

The simplest harmonic sequence is:

1, 1/2, 1/3, 1/4, 1/5, …

Taking reciprocals of each term gives us the arithmetic sequence:

1, 2, 3, 4, 5, …
